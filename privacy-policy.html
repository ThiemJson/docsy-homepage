Docsy – Document Scanner Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Docsy – Document Scanner (“we”, “our”, or “the app”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data.

1. Overview

Docsy is designed with a privacy-first approach.

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

All document scanning, processing, and storage happens entirely on your device.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect any personal information, including:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics

Docsy does not require account registration or login.

3. Data Storage

All data created within the app, including:

  • Scanned documents
  • Signatures
  • Collections and metadata

is stored locally on your device only.

We do not upload or back up your data to any server.

4. Permissions

Docsy may request the following permissions:

Camera

Used to scan documents using your device camera.

Photo Library (Read & Write)

Used to:

  • Import images for scanning
  • Save scanned documents to your device

These permissions are used only for core app functionality.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share any data with:

  • Third-party services
  • Analytics providers
  • Advertisers

Your data is only shared when you explicitly choose to export or share documents (e.g., via email, messaging apps, or cloud storage).

6. Third-Party Services

Docsy does not integrate any third-party SDKs that collect user data.

7. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Your data is protected by your device’s security (passcode, biometrics, encryption)
  • We do not have access to your files

8. Data Deletion

You have full control over your data:

  • Delete documents or collections داخل app
  • Uninstall the app to remove all data permanently

9. Children’s Privacy

Docsy does not knowingly collect any data from children under the age of 13.

Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.
